Edit Administrators (Authors)


With this special feature, you can add, modify and delete administrators.

Add a new administrator :
Fill out the form. Mandatory fields are marked with an * (Nickname, Name, Email, Password).
Other fields are not mandatory. Then pick what kind of features this administrator will handle.
Click on Add an Author/Admin to complete this action.

Edit an administrator :
Click on Modify to modify users info/profile. Change what you need to change and click on Refresh Author.

Delete an administrator :
Click on Delete Author and validate the message.

Nota bene : NPDS requires at least one administrator to run.
