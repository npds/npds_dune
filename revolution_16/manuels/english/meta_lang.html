META_LANG ?


To what that is of use ?
The META_LANG is particular expressions of the NPDS language.
They allow, safely, to call up to the place or you registered this META_LANG (in a new, an article, a note), a comment or the édito of your site to see even a page static), the result of evolved function appropriate for the NPDS language.

  3. smil
