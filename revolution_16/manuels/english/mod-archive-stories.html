Articles archives

This core module allows a summary display of articles. It is installed by default and therefore available.

You can define here:

  • the header of the page:
    everything that will be located just above the presentation table. (html and multi-language supported)
  • the type of article displayed:
    - online articles
    or
    - archived articles
  • the number of articles in the presentation table
  • the page cache retention time (in supercache, operational when supercache is enabled)
