Submissions


This is where you manage the articles posted by the users and wether you want to publish, edit or delete them

The first option allows to delete this submision. When you click on delete, the MySQL DB update is in realtime! Be careful.

You can also automate any kind of the submissions. Click on Auto to get to the "Automated articles" (date and hour setup...) to publish the article on the given date.

If you want to publish the article right away, click on its title, edit the required fields and agree to submit it.
