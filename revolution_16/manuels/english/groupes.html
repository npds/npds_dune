Groups Management


This module allows you to manage groups for members of your website.

List groups and members affiliated to groups :
 - In red are the groups that do not have members yet or whose members do no longer exist.
 - You can edit or modify informations related to groups (which doesn't delete groups).

You have to create a group and then set the group attachement to the user..

If you need to delete a group, delete all members first (from the group)

ID of group must be between 2 and 126

If you want to add several members in a group, separate pseudo by , (coma) in the field
