Poll / Survey


Change your polls frequently! Think about the users!
To add a new poll, just fill out the form from the Poll module.

You can have several Polls "running" on your website (several blocks / pollnewest params) AND polls in static page (for example) with meta-mot !block!... and metalang=1 option of static.php.

Title :
The question of your poll.

Options :
A poll requires a minimum of two options (yes /no) and you can use all the fields but it is not mandatory.

Delete a poll :
Click on delete polls. Then choose which poll you want to delete. The Database will delete the poll right away so there is no turning back! Be careful!

Edit a poll :
You can edit a poll but it won't change the votes. Select the poll and click on Edit.
You can set it to members-only and/or close the poll. The database is updated right away, there's no turning back.
