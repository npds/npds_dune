Surveys/polls

Make the effort to change your surveys frequently!
To add a new survey, simply fill out the administration form.

You can also have several polls in service on your site (several blocks / configuration of the pollnewest function), see polls in static pages (via the meta-mot !block!... and the metalang= option 1 of static.php).

Survey title:

The title you chose for your survey.

Options:

A survey must have at least 2 options and you do not have to use them all.

Delete a survey:

To delete one or more existing surveys, click the Clear Surveys link. You then arrive on a page allowing you to choose the survey you wish to delete.
Choose the survey and click on the Remove button. Please note that the database update is Immediate.

Edit a survey:

You can edit your poll, but be careful this will not affect the votes. Choose the survey and click on the Edit button.
You can reserve a poll for members and/or close votes. Please note that the database update is Immediate.
