SuperCache

This module allows to manage a powerful feature of NPDS

SuperCache

SuperCache is a Php/Html cache/buffer system that let you optimize your website performances !

You can:

  • Turn On/Off Supercache
  • Define the values, buffering time (in seconds)
  • Empty the cache directories when massively updating your site.
