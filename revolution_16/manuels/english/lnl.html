Newsletter

This module allows you to manage a Newsletter for your site.

The main functions of this module are:

  • Management of a Newsletter in 3 parts: header, body, footer
  • Management of a Newsletter in text or html mode
  • Manage shipments for members, prospects or both
  • Monitoring sent LNLs and prospects list

Typically, the mode of operation is:

  • Creating a Header (Text or Html)
  • Creating a Body (Text or Html)
  • Creating a Footer (Text or Html)
  • LNL test (enter the number for each element)
    This test allows you to validate the rendering of your LNL
  • Sending the LNL (enter the subject and recipients).

You can add, modify or delete the "components" of your LNL.

You have a status of the LNLs sent with a certain amount of information and in particular the Type:

  • All => LNL sent to all members and all prospects,
  • Mbr => members only
  • Out => only to prospects
as well as the list of prospects (Status OK => The user wishes to receive the Letter, NOK, he does not wish).

Of course, the email function must be active with your host to be able to use this functionality.

Above 50 members, the LNL will be sent in "packets" of 50 every 10 seconds, thus avoiding saturating your mail server.
