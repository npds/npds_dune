LNL - Little NewsLetter


This module lets you manage a little newsletter for your site.

Its features are :
 - Management of LNL in 3 parts : header, body, footer,
 - Management of LNL in plain text or HTML,
 - LNL for members or/and anonymous that left their email adress using the lnl-block,
 - LNL'status.

Basically it works this way :
 - Creation of a header (Text or Html),
 - Creation of a body (Text or Html),
 - Creation of a footer (Text or Html),
 - Test LNL (enter a number for each of the elements),
   This test lets you preview the Newsletter
 - Send LNL (enter subject and members to send it to).

You can add, modify or delete some parts of your Little NewsLetter.

You can see the status of LNL already sent and in particular the following types :  
- All => LNL send to all members and anonymous that left their email adress using the lnl-block,
- Mbr => sent only to members,
- Out => only anonymous that left their email adress using the lnl-block,

Also anonymous status (OK => Send me the LNL, NOK else).

Of course, the Mail (or Email) function has to be activated by your hosting provider so you can use this feature.
Above 50 members, the LNL will be sent by group of 50 every 10 seconds, this won't saturate your messagery server.
